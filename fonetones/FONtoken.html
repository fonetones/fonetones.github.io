Most recent White Paper.

A Liquidity Pool (LP) between ETH and FON on the Ethereum blockchain at Uniswap was formed. The idea is that arbitrage can happen between Solana and Ethereum by selling on the one chain and buying on the other chain, to get the prices the same, because according to the whitepaper, the tokens on Solana and Ethereum have the same value. On 3 August 2025 the buy price on Ethereum was about 0.000002 USD/FON and on Solana 0.000005 USD/FON. That means, then, FON could be bought on the Ethereum blockchain for about half the price on the Solana Chain.

Arbitrage trading between the two chains can cause profit and supply market making at the same time.

The PumpFun liquidity pool (LP) and Solana holders, hold 1'000'000'000 (one billion) FON. On 16 August 2025 the Uniswap LP had about 100'000'000 (one hundred million) FON in it. Africahead, the developer of FON, will sell FON into the Uniswap LP, to fill it with 1'000'000'000 (one billion) FON, as on PumpSwap. Selling into the Uniswap LP will raise the capital to build the fonetones brand with further.
